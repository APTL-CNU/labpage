Prof. Seho Kweon

Assistant Professor

College of Pharmacy · Chonnam National University

Seho Kweon is an assistant Professor at the College of Pharmacy at Chonnam National University in South Korea. With a Ph.D. from Seoul National University and having research experience at Biomedicine Cutting Edge Formulation Technology Center, he possesses extensive expertise. Before joining academia, he contributed significantly to drug development and regulatory processes at Hanmi Pharmaceuticals Co. Ltd. His current research focuses on developing computational pharmaceutics for biologics, peptide delivery systems for metabolic disease, and applying mathematical models to cancer treatment for translational science.

Research Interests
Computational Drug Discovery Peptide engineering Drug Delivery System Biopharmaceutics Nanoformulation Oral delivery
